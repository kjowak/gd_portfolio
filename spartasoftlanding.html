Spartasoft Studio

The Future of my Game Development Journey

Provide, Educate, Create

Spartasoft Studio is a student-ran Game Development team at MSU. Its goals are to:

  1. Provide a space to develop games in a large group setting
  2. Educate students through experience about the game development pipeline
  3. and Create projects our team feels passionate about
Each semester, the team works on a project from start to finish, giving students the opportunity to develop their game development skills at every stage of the game creation process. While I've only been on the team for a single semester, I can proudly say that I was one of two programmers nominated for the Team Director position, and look forward to working on more projects in the future.

Tomb For Two - 2022

Tomb For Two was the Fall 2022 Spartasoft Studio project. It was my first experience working on a game development team, as well as my first experience in Unity.
